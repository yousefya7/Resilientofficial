Lastly, the company did not have strong protection against DDOS attacks, which allowed attackers to bring their website down. 

Question 2: HealthPlus could have prevented it by implementing stronger login secuirty. Multi factor authentication requires users to verify their identity using a second method like a code sent to their phone. This would have made it a lot harder for any attackers to access accounts even with stolen passwords. They could also add regular cybersecurity training for employees so they can recognize phishing emails suspicious logins. Lastly, email filtering systems could be used to detect & block messages before they reach employees.
